THE MAKING OF "RANDOM RUNNER"

MAKING "RANDOM RUNNER" WAS OUR PROJECT FOR TERM 2 OF OUR DESIGN CLASS.

Random runer was a game created by 2021-2022 grade 9 design coding students, Morgan Flanagan, Bianca Pizzuco, and Sofie Sondermeyer.


To create this game, we used two programs, Game Maker and Visual Code Sudio
