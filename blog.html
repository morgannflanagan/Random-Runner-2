Please ask any questions you have about our game


What do I do if the game freezes?

If your page freezes, simply just refresh the page!


How do the points work?

To gain points, you must run throughout the entire map and jump over the objects coming towards you.


How do I move Kiki?

To move the character, you must use the arrow keys. The up arrow is used to jump over the objects.
